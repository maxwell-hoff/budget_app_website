What's on the workbench

Serious tools, made approachable.

Everything you need to plan deliberately — the kind of capabilities professionals rely on, distilled into something you'll actually want to open.

01

Scenario Planning

  • Save different paths as scenarios.
  • Easily compare scenarios side-by-side.
02

Fine-tuned Forecasting

  • Adjust parameters at an event level.
  • Create parameters to apply to all events.
03

Budget vs. Actual Insights

  • Understand the root causes of overspending.
04

Privacy-First by Default

  • Your financial data stays on your device, not on someone else's server.
05

All Accounts in One Place

  • View consolidated balances from many institutions.
  • Create spouse profiles and plan together.
06

Advanced Accounting Tools

  • Mark transactions as transfers between accounts.
  • Spread out payments over time.
  • Split transactions across multiple accounts.
