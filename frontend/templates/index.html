The fix

From messy transactions to a number you trust — and the tools that get you there.

Here's how Workbench fixes that. A guided setup walks you in from zero — plain-language questions, no blank screen, your real numbers in about five minutes — and from there you're in a loop. Each step below is a stage; the tools beneath it are what you use to get through it, every one built to show you the downstream price of a decision before you commit. Not just what you spent, but what it compounds to.

Our philosophy

Understanding can't be handed to you.

There's a Taoist idea that the knowledge in a craftsman's hands can't be passed on in words — it has to be worked for. Finances are the same. You can read reports, get advice, follow a plan. But knowing what your money is doing and where it's taking you only comes from working with it directly. So we built a tool that rewards that — clean accounting underneath, scenario planning on top, everything on your machine.

Built in the open

Workbench is being built in public. Every trade-off, dead end, and shipped feature is out where you can see it — no manufactured reviews, no growth-hacked hype. Watch it take shape, kick the tires, and help decide where it goes next.
