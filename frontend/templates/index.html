The future of personal budgeting

Budgets don't fail because you overspend. They fail because you can't see what overspending costs.

Workbench connects your spending to its consequences. Change one assumption — a raise, a new expense, a cutback — and see what it does to your financial picture months from now. When the tradeoff is visible, the decision changes. Built with serious accounting tools underneath so the numbers you're planning from are actually clean.

Our philosophy

Understanding can't be handed to you.

Most budgeting tools do the work for you — categorize your spending, summarize your month, tell you where you went over. But a summary of what happened isn't the same as understanding where you're headed. That only comes from working with the numbers yourself, adjusting assumptions, seeing what changes. So we built a tool that makes that worth doing, not one that tries to do the understanding for you.
